WHAT IS SPECIAL ABOUT COFFEE?

According to the global coffee exchange, coffee is the US's largest food import and second most valuable commodity only after oil. According to the International Coffee Organization, the US imported 2.72 billion pounds of coffee from September 2001 to September 2002.(Global coffee Exchange 2011)
